Heritage

A Story of Passion & Precision

From humble beginnings to global recognition, our journey has been defined by unwavering commitment to excellence and the art of fine jewelry making.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1990 by master jeweler Alexander Windsor, our journey began in a small workshop in London with a singular vision: to create jewelry that transcends time and trends. Today, three generations later, we continue to uphold the traditions of excellence while embracing innovation.

Every piece that leaves our atelier represents over 30 years of perfected techniques, passed down through generations of master jewelers. We source only the finest materials from ethical suppliers, ensuring that each creation not only looks exquisite but also carries a story of responsibility and sustainability.

From engagement rings that mark new beginnings to heirloom pieces that connect generations, ROYAL HERITAGE has been privileged to be part of life's most precious moments. Our commitment to quality, design, and customer satisfaction remains unwavering.

Our Core Values

Partners

Our Contributing Brands

Prestigious luxury brands that form the cornerstone of our exceptional collection of fine jewelry.

Sterling Jewels

Known for their contemporary designs and exceptional craftsmanship, Sterling Jewels brings modern elegance to traditional jewelry making. Their pieces feature innovative settings and unique gemstone combinations that appeal to the discerning collector.

Royal Crest

A heritage brand with over a century of experience, Royal Crest specializes in regal pieces that embody timeless sophistication. Their collections are inspired by royal jewelry traditions and feature only the finest precious metals and gemstones.

Windsor & Co.

Founded by our own Alexander Windsor, this brand represents the pinnacle of our craftsmanship. Windsor & Co. creates bespoke pieces that combine traditional techniques with avant-garde design, resulting in truly unique jewelry.

Celestial Jewels

Celestial Jewels is celebrated for their ethereal designs that capture the essence of celestial beauty. Their pieces often feature delicate motifs inspired by angels and stars, creating jewelry that feels both divine and wearable.

Lunar Atelier

Lunar Atelier draws inspiration from the mysteries of the night sky, creating pieces that capture the magic of moonlight and stars. Their distinctive style combines celestial motifs with innovative materials.
